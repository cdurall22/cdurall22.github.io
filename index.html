What is Anime? What makes Anime, Anime.

Anime is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from Japan. Anime, a term derived from the English word animation, is used in Japanese to describe all animation, regardless of style or origin.

In Japan all animation, regardless of kind, style, or where it was made, is anime. Anime is simply the word Japan has adapted to use to refer to animated cartoons. ... Outside Japan anime typically only refers to animation from Japan. Some argue style is involved as well, but there is no one single “anime style” out there.
